Preparation Guide

STEP 1

COOK

Heat a lightly greased grill or grill pan to medium high heat.

STEP 2

SHRED

When chicken is cool enough to handle, shred into bite size pieces. Toss with the barbecue sauce and set aside.

TIP

TIP: Shredding with your hands give you chicken chunks. Shredding with two forks give you fluffy shreds.

STEP 3

ROLL

Onto a floured counter, roll the pizza dough into a 12-inch round or rectangle.

STEP 4

GRILL

Heat a grill or grill pan to medium heat. Lightly brush one side of the pizza dough with half a tablespoon of olive oil.

STEP 5

SERVE

Sprinkle pizza with shredded cheese while it's still on the grill.
